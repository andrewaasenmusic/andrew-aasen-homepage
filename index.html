About Andrew
Andrew Aasen is a genre-fluid artist and producer based between Bend, Kaua‘i, and Paris. Known for merging digital soul with ambient pop and glitchy elegance, he curates and produces for a growing roster of artists under The Collective — including Echo, Avi Lux, Big Eddy, and Alexandra Glücksburg.

With over a million streams and a magnetic creative voice, Andrew blurs the line between high concept and headphones. He doesn’t chase trends — he rewrites them.
