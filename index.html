About
Andrew Aasen is a multi-genre producer and artist based between Bend, Oregon, Kaua‘i, and Paris. Known for merging digital soul with ambient pop and subtle techno flavors, Andrew curates The Collective—featuring exclusive artists Echo, Avi Lux, Alexandra Glücksburg, and Big Eddy.

With over a million streams and a unique vision for independent artistry, he’s redefined what it means to be both creator and curator. From luxe rooftops to moody studios, Andrew's work speaks with bold softness.
